Features

Everything you need

Built for DU students, by a DU student. Simple, fast, and useful.

🗺️
Live GPS Tracking
See where buses are right now on a live map. Location data shared by riders on the bus.
👨‍✈️
Driver Mode
A simplified interface for drivers with distraction-free access to maps and schedules.
🕐
Full Schedule
Complete timetables for all 17+ DU bus routes with stop-wise arrival estimates.
📍
Stop Maps
Interactive maps showing every stop for every route. Know exactly where to wait.
📢
Notices
General and bus-specific announcements. Admins can post, students stay informed.
🔐
DU Auth
Login with your @du.ac.bd Google account or traditional email/password.
